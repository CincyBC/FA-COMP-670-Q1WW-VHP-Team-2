  Learn to lead with a career-focused business degree



From customer demographics to enterprise mergers to transformative business models, organizations rely on those who can identify opportunities, develop global-minded solutions, create impact and leave a legacy.
 
The business degree programs through Franklin University’s Ross College of Business can help equip you to respond to global business challenges. Learn how to enhance your ability to think critically, plan strategically and execute creatively.
More importantly, discover what it takes to become an adaptive professional, delivering breakthrough business solutions. Find your path.

  Specialized Accreditation







  International Accreditation Council for Business Education (IACBE)
The Ross College of Business at Franklin University has received specialized accreditation for its business programs through the International Accreditation Council for Business Education (IACBE) located at 11960 Quivira Road in Overland Park, Kansas, USA. For a list of accredited programs please view our IACBE member status page. Click here to see our IACBE Public Disclosure of Student Achievement. For more information about the International Accreditation Council for Business Education, please click here or visit www.iacbe.org.
