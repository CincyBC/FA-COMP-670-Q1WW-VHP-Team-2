Showcase your creativity with an online degree in marketing & communications

Energize yourself and your career with a marketing & communications degree earned 100% online from Franklin. If you're looking for a career that lets you explore new ways to help organizations spread ideas, share information, and influence others, then you need a marketing & communications degree. We offer affordable and convenient online marketing and communications programs, including undergraduate and graduate-level degrees.
