Military Family Member Discount

Are you the spouse or dependent child of a current member of the U.S. Armed Forces? Your family member sacrifices a lot for our country - and so do you.

That's why Franklin offers you a 20% discount on your associate, bachelor's master's, or certificate program. Even with the unique challenges of a military family, Franklin makes continuing your education both accessible and affordable. 

Complete the Military Family Member Tuition Reduction Application to apply.
