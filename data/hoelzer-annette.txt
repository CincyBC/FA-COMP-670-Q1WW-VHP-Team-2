        Hoelzer, Annette
        






Profile
I was tax accountant in public accounting for 33 years. Prior to that, I taught for 8 years. I have been teaching at Franklin University for 6 years and am currently serving as the  Lead Faculty for Forensic Accounting. One of my greatest joys in life is guiding future leaders in the accounting profession.
